CineSelect Site Plan

Site Name

CineSelect

The name “CineSelect” combines “Cinema” and “Select,” highlighting the site’s purpose of allowing users to select movies and seats easily.

Site Purpose

CineSelect is an online movie ticketing platform where users browse currently showing movies, select their preferred movie and seats, then complete their purchase with personal and payment information.

Scenarios

  • How can I find and select a movie playing this weekend?
  • Can I choose my preferred seats before purchasing tickets?
  • What information do I need to provide to complete my ticket purchase?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #E50914 — used for headings, buttons, and call-to-action elements.

Secondary Color: #1F1F1F — used for backgrounds and body text to evoke a cinematic feel.

Typography

Montserrat — used for headings and navigation for a bold, modern look.

Open Sans — used for body text and forms to ensure readability.
